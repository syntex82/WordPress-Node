Android App Installation

Step-by-step guide

Installation Steps

1

Download the APK

Tap the download button below to get the app file.

2

Allow Download

When Chrome shows "This type of file can harm your device", tap Download anyway. The app is digitally signed and safe.

3

Open the File

Tap the notification when download completes, or find learn-online.apk in your Downloads folder.

4

Enable Installation

If prompted, go to Settings and enable "Allow from this source" for your browser. This is required for apps outside Play Store.

5

Install & Open

Tap Install, wait for completion, then tap Open to launch the app.
